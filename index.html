About Us

Berry Bakery was founded in Jakarta, 2003, by Allie Allison together with Chef Harry Harrison. As the time passed, we recruited more staff and chefs. Until now, there are about 40 staff and 5 chefs who joined our family. In 2007, we were proudly opened a new branch store in Bandung.
